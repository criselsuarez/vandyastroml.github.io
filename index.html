General Information

This is a repository of the different Seminars and Workshops taught by the people of the Vanderbilt Astronomy Group.

The main purpose of these seminars and workshops are:

  • Learn new tools and applications that can later be used in research
  • Create a space for answering computer-related questions
  • Help each other solve research problems

Seminars and Workshops

  • Fall 2021 - Vanderbilt-Fisk Computational Bootcamp
  • Fall 2020 - Vanderbilt-Fisk Computational Bootcamp
  • Fall 2019 - Vanderbilt-Fisk Computational Bootcamp
  • Fall 2018 - Vanderbilt-Fisk Computational Bootcamp
  • Spring 2017 - Computational Workshop
  • Spring 2017 - Graduate Professional Seminar
  • Spring 2016 - Astro-ML
